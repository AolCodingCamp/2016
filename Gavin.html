Introduction

Hello, my name is Gavin. I am 12 years old and this is my page of the website we're making at this AOL camp.

My main project was called echo. I did it with my two friends Shane and Ben. It was to help blind people get around.They could "feel" when they were coming close to someone or something.

How it worked.

Echo used Ultrasonic sensors to tell how far away an object was and vibrated the closer you got. It was mounted on a wristband so you could point your hand a something and tell how far away it was. It was all controlled by a lilypad arduino.
